Features
Odin Tools makes managing your Torn.com faction easier and more efficient. Here's what it offers:
Target & War Target Lists:
Keep track of up to 50 (soon more) personal targets, or war targets in one place.
See key details like hospital timers, life status, and recent respect gains.
Easy links to profiles and one-click attack options.
Faction Member Overview:
View your faction members at a glance with status icons (online, idle, offline).
Sort the list by level, role, time in faction, or activity.
Search for specific members and spot who's available.
Enemy Faction Watchlist:
Add an enemy faction by ID to monitor their members.
Chain Alerts & Reminders:
Get notifications when chain time is running low, or bonuses are close (within 20/10 of bonuses 500 and up.
Visual warningstso you dont miss it.
and popup notifications to slow down, read faction chat, or to stop hits within 10 hits of a bonus and watch faction chat. 
Alerts for when too many enemies are online at 1 time, alerting to a possible enemy push.
Direct access to your faction's medical supplies from the drawer.
Personalized Look:
Move and resize the side panel to fit your screen.
Choose from fun themes with neon effects, fonts, and colors.
Works great on mobile with easy-to-read tables.

How to Install

Odin Tools works as a simple add-on in your browser. 
What You Need:
Any modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge.
The Tampermonkey add-on from tampermonkey.net.
Easy Setup
Add the Script:
Open Tampermonkey (click its icon in your browser toolbar).
Hit the "+" to make a new script.
Clear the box and paste the code, or click the download link.
Save it (Ctrl+S on Windows, Cmd+S on Mac).
Head to Torn:
Go to www.torn.com and refresh the page.
The script turns on for Torn pages automatically.
Set It Up:
A quick popup asks for your Torn API key, Full Access is required to pull data (the script is 100% localcto your browser, the only feature that talks outside of torn.com is sending Bjorn error reports, which do not include the API key, or personal Data.
Enter it once—it's saved safely in your browser.
If Something's Off:
Check for error messages in your browser's console (press F12).
Reload Tampermonkey's list and make sure the script is checked on.
Updates happen when you save—Tampermonkey handles the rest.
